About Us

Vyanjan, initially a fast food venture in 2016 that provided mainly Chinese and some fast foods, has come a long way to transform into a premium restaurant well-known for a wide diversity of its cuisine, which includes Italian, French, Mexican, South Indian and a lot more; making it one of the few restaurants in Punjab that offer French dishes. In 2019, Vyanjan made its way into the list of the Top 20 restuarants of India and Top 5 restaurants of Punjab.

Vyanjan believes in delivering wide varities of food without compromising quality and taste, giving utmost satisfaction to our beloved customers with the help of our special secret ingredient: Love.
